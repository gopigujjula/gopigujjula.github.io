Sitecore Developer Community in India

October 20, 2018
Sitecore, Sitecore Community

Best way to connect with Sitecore community across the globe is through Sitecore Slack or Sitecore Stack Exchange, or Community Site. You can find ways to connect, and further information here.

But there are also large pool of Sitecore developers in India, who connect through local community groups, here I am providing more information about Sitecore user groups in India.

SUGCON India (Sitecore User Group Conference India):


This is the wonderful conference where you can watch awesome presentations & network with all the Sitecore developers from India at one place. First ever conference happened on May-2018. Look out for next year conference, and find more details here.

Important points:

  • User group name itself suggests you, in which city it is organized. (only exception is SUG India, it is run from Vadodara, Gujarat)
  • All the above meetups are free of cost to attend. In some of the meetups you can also collect goodies from sponsors. (Obviously except the SUGCON India, it is a conference, paid one, but ticket cost is very less compared to what it offers.)
  • All the user groups are continuously looking for speakers. If you want to present either remotely or in-person, do reach out to organizers immediately.
  • Most of the user groups record their sessions and put it on YouTube. So you can also watch previous meetup sessions.
  • I have provided all the above information to best of my knowledge.

Happy Learning :)
