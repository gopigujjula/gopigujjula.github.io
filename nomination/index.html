Motivation, why you deserve this

Couple of years back, got an opportunity to work on Sitecore at Verndale, I took the chance, and very much glad that I did it. When I started with my first Sitecore implmentation, there are struggles, but with the help of my colleagues, and the wonderful Sitecore community, improved day by day in understanding the platform, and improving my skills.

But later noticed, outside the Sitecore community, it was little bit difficult for .NET developers to understand what exactly is Sitecore. This is where decided to share my notes (prepared notes, while I was learning Sitecore) as blog posts. Then the magic happened, I used to get lot of emails, and lot of .NET developers started reaching out to me, to clarify their basic doubts on the platform. Regularly, I try to answer all the basic questions, and also created couple of video tutorials to help in the same area. My blogging journey continues, I try to share whatever things I learn on the platform.

Gave my first ever technical presentation at local SUG, that is when, my journey started with SUG Bangalore, now I am also co-organizer for the group, play key role in organizing the in-person meetups. Later got the opportunity to travel, and present at SUG Hyderabad, I had also presented at local .NET User Group on Introduction to Sitecore (to around 90+ .NET developers).

Till now gave 9 tech talks in three user groups, and next session is on the cards with SUG India, that would be mostly in December.

I created Sitecore Bangalore WhatsApp group to bring all the local Sitecore developers under one roof. This group is very active, now we are 75+ developers from 15+ organizations. We share and discuss all the things around Sitecore, and also it has become good extension for SUG Bangalore, it is for the developers, by the developers. This group also became a place for developers to post about any job openings in their team.

I enjoy being part of the community, be it blogging, presenting, or talking to other developers, and I believe, sharing is the key to improve the knowledge.

Objectives for next year

I plan to continue blogging, and presenting. I also plan to record more and more video tutorials, and help other developers. Also from SUG Bangalore side, want to continue successfully organizing meetups, may be try to increase the frequency of the meetups.
From learning aspect, want to learn and explore all the things in Sitecore 9, and increase knowledge on Sitecore commerce.
